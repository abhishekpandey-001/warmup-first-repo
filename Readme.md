### 🔹 FloCard
- It's a digital networking platform to connect individuals, brands, and organizations.
- It replaces traditional paper visiting cards.
- Supports environmental sustainability by planting a tree for every FloCard created.
- Allows users to:
  - Create multiple cards
  - Share via QR or code
  - Manage connections and communicate

---

### 🔹 Ecosystem Understanding

- **Communities**: Collaborate and work together in groups.
- **Tree Planters**: Track tree growth, monitor impact, and manage plantation projects.

## 📅 Day 2 – Product Deep Dive & Tools

### 🔹 KidCare (Overview)
KidCare is a hybrid (digital + physical) healthcare system for children that connects:
- Parents
- Hospitals
- Doctors
- Care Partners

---

### 🔹 Parent Perspective
- Add up to 4 children
- Track:
  - Growth charts (height, weight, BMI)
  - Diet plans
  - Prescriptions
  - Appointments
- Book appointments via app (online or walk-in)
- Use **Flip** feature for:
  - Emergency
  - Queries
  - Feedback

---

### 🔹 Management Perspective
- Access all patient data
- Monitor appointments and health records
- Respond to emergencies (Flip)
- Suppport decision-making with shared data

---

### 🔹 Core Value of KidCare
- Simplifies child healthcare management
- Ensures transparency between parents and hospitals
- Enables digital tracking and communication
